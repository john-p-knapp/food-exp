# Greek Yogurt Marinated Chicken 

### Ingredients: 
* 1 cup greek yogurt
* 3 tbl olive oil
* 2 tbl lemon juice
* 3 cloves garlic
* 1 tsp oregano
* 1 tsp cumin
* 1 tsp smoked paprika
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp pepper
* 1 lbs chicken breast

### Directions: 

#### Marinade {
1. In a large bowl whisk together **1 cup greek yogurt** , **3 tbl olive oil** , **2 tbl lemon juice** , **3 cloves garlic , minced** , **1 tsp dried oregano** , **1 tsp cumin** , **1 tsp smoked paprika** , **1/2 tsp salt** , and **1/2 tsp pepper** . 

}

1. Portion **1 lbs chicken breast** and coat with **Marinade** s. 
2. Refrigerate at least 1 hour, preferably 4 hours or overnight. 
3. Grill 
4. Serve garnished with lemon wedges
