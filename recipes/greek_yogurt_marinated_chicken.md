# Greek Yogurt Marinated Chicken 

* https://savorfield.com/recipe/greek-yogurt-marinated-chicken/
### Ingredients: 
* 1 cup greek yogurt
* 3 tbl olive oil
* 2 tbl lemon juice
* 3 cloves garlic
* 1 tsp oregano
* 1 tsp cumin
* 1 tsp smoked paprika
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp pepper
* 1 lbs chicken breast
*  fresh parsley
*  lemon wedges

### Directions: 

#### Marinade {
1. In a large bowl whisk together **1 cup greek yogurt** , **3 tbl olive oil** , **2 tbl lemon juice** , **3 cloves garlic , minced** , **1 tsp dried oregano** , **1 tsp cumin** , **1 tsp smoked paprika** , **1/2 tsp salt** , and **1/2 tsp pepper** until smooth. 

}

1. Coat **1 lbs chicken breast** with **Marinade** in a resealable bag or shallow dish. 
2. Refrigerate at least 1 hour, preferably 4 hours or overnight. 
3. Preheat grill or oven to medium-high heat; lightly oil the grates if grilling. 
4. Remove chicken from marinade, letting excess drip off; discard used marinade. 
5. Grill 6-7 minutes per side, or bake at 400F for 25-30 minutes, until cooked through and the internal temp reaches 165F. 
6. Let rest 5 minutes before slicing. 
7. Serve garnished with chopped **fresh parsley** and **lemon wedges** to squeeze over the top.
