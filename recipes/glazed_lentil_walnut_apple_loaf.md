# Glazed Lentil Walnut Apple Loaf 

### Ingredients: 
* 1/4 cup ketchup
* 1 tbl maple syrup
* 2 tbl apple butter
* 2 tbl balsamic vinegar
* 1 cup green lentils
* 3 cup water
*  salt
* 1 cup pecans
* 3 tbl ground flax
* 1/2 cup water
* 3 cloves garlic
* 1.5 cup sweet onion
*  salt
* 1 cup celery
* 1 cup carrot
* 1/3 cup apple
* 1/3 cup raisins
* 1/2 cup oat flour
* 3/4 cup breadcrumbs
* 2 tsp thyme
*  pepper
*  aleppo

### Directions: 

#### Balsamic Apple Glaze {
1. Whisk together **1/4 cup ketchup** , **1 tbl maple syrup** , **2 tbl apple butter** and **2 tbl balsamic vinegar** . 

}

1. Pick over and rinse **1 cup green lentils** . Add **3 cup water** , season with **salt** Simmer, uncovered, at least 40 to 45 minutes, stirring frequently and adding a touch of water if needed; the goal is to slightly overcook them. Mash the lentils slightly with a spoon when ready. 
2. Preheat the oven to 325F. 
3. Toast **1 cup pecans** at 325F for about 7 minutes; chop and set aside. 
4. Increase the oven temperature to 350F. 
5. Whisk **3 tbl ground flax** with **1/2 cup water** in a small bowl and set aside. 
6. Saute **3 cloves garlic , minced** and **1.5 cup diced sweet onion** until softened; season with **salt** . 
7. Add **1 cup diced celery** , **1 cup grated carrot** , **1/3 cup peeled and grated apple** and **1/3 cup raisins** , and saute about 5 minutes more. Remove from the heat. 
8. In a large mixing bowl combine:  
**1/2 cup oat flour**   
**3/4 cup breadcrumbs**   
**2 tsp thyme**   
**pepper**   
**aleppo**   


9. Add the cooked lentils, toasted nuts, flax mixture and sauteed vegetables; mix. 
10. Grease a loaf pan and line it with parchment paper. 
11. Press the mixture firmly into the pan. 
12. Spread half of the **Balsamic Apple Glaze** over the top of the loaf and reserve the rest as a dipping sauce. 
13. Bake at 350F for 40 to 50 minutes, uncovered, until the edges are lightly browned. 
14. Cool in the pan at least 10 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack; wait until fully cool before slicing.
