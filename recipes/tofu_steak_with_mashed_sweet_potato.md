# Tofu Steak with Mashed Sweet Potato 

### Ingredients: 
* 15 oz extra firm tofu
* 2 tbl tamari
* 2 tbl maple syrup
* 2 tbl mirin
* 1 tbl toasted sesame oil
* 1 tbl barbecue sauce
* 1 tbl sriracha
* 1 cloves garlic
* 1 tsp garlic powder
* 1/2 tsp liquid smoke
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
* 2 lbs sweet potatoes
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/4 cup milk
* 3 tbl butter
*  salt
*  pepper

### Directions: 

#### Tofu {
1. Press **15 oz extra firm tofu** and slice into 8 slabs. 
2. In a shallow dish that will hold the tofu combine:  
**2 tbl tamari**   
**2 tbl maple syrup**   
**2 tbl mirin**   
**1 tbl toasted sesame oil**   
**1 tbl barbecue sauce**   
**1 tbl sriracha**   
**1 cloves garlic , crushed**   
**1 tsp garlic powder**   
**1/2 tsp liquid smoke**   
**1/2 tsp salt**   
**1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper**   


3. Place tofu slices in the dish and toss to coat. Marinate for at least 15 minutes. 
4. Pan fry tofu. 

}


#### Sweet Potato Mash {
1. Peel and cube **2 lbs sweet potatoes** . 
2. Gently boil sweet potatoes until very tender. 
3. Drain and add: **1 tsp salt** , **1/4 cup milk** , **3 tbl butter** . 
4. Mash and stir to combine. 
5. Season with **salt** and **pepper** . 

}

1. Serve tofu over a bed of sweet potato mash. Brush tofu with extra marinade if desired.
