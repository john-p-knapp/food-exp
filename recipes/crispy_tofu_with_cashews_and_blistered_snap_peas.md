# Crispy Tofu With Cashews and Blistered Snap Peas 

* Tofu may be browned either on the stove or in the oven depending on your preference. 
### Ingredients: 
* 14 oz extra firm tofu
* 12 oz snap peas
* 2 tbl ginger
* 2 cloves garlic
* 1/2 cup cashew nuts
* 1 tbl soy sauce
* 2 tsp molasses
* 4 scallion
* 1/4 cup mint
*  rice
* 1 tbl oil
*  salt
*  pepper
* 1 tbl oil
* 1 tbl oil
* 14 oz coconut milk
* 1 tbl rice vinegar

### Directions: 

#### Mis en place {
1. Press **14 oz extra firm tofu** . 
2. Trim **12 oz snap peas** . 
3. Grate **2 tbl ginger** and **2 cloves garlic** . 
4. Toast and chop **1/2 cup cashew nuts** 
5. In a small prep bowl combine:  
**1 tbl soy sauce**   
**2 tsp molasses**   


6. Slice **4 scallion** and **1/4 cup mint** . 

}

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees if baking the tofu. 
2. Prepare **rice** if desired. 
3. Slice the tofu in half horizontally and cut into 1 inch pieces. Toss with **1 tbl oil** . Season with **salt** and **pepper** . 
4. Place tofu on a rimmed baking sheet and bake until lightly crisp (or pan fry if desired). 
5. In a heavy skillet heat **1 tbl oil** , add snap peas. Cook, stirring occasionally, until blistered and just tender. Season with salt and remove to a bowl. 
6. Heat **1 tbl oil** in the pan and cook the ginger and garlic until fragrant. 
7. Pour in **14 oz coconut milk** , soy sauce mixture. Simmer, stirring frequently until the sauce reduces and its color deepens. It should coat a spoon. 
8. Add tofu and cashews to the pan and combine. 
9. Toss the snap peas with **1 tbl rice vinegar** , scallions, mint and 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes. 
10. Serve tofu with snap peas and rice.
