# Harissa Honey Chicken Skewers 

### Ingredients: 
* 3 tbl harissa paste
* 3 tbl honey
* 3 tbl olive oil
* 2 tbl lemon juice
* 3 cloves garlic
* 1.5 tsp cumin
* 1.5 tsp paprika
* 1 tsp salt
* 1.5 lbs chicken breast

### Directions: 
1. In a large bowl whisk to combine:  
**3 tbl harissa paste**   
**3 tbl honey**   
**3 tbl olive oil**   
**2 tbl lemon juice**
