# Tofu Basil Pesto Pasta 

### Ingredients: 
* 1 cup silken tofu
* 2 tbl olive oil
* 1 cloves garlic
* 1 cup basil
* 1/2 tsp black pepper
* 4 oz parmigiano
* 2 tbl lemon juice
* 1/4 tsp salt
* 2 cup penne pasta
* 1/4 cup shallot

### Directions: 
1. In a food processor combine:  
**1 cup silken tofu**   
**2 tbl olive oil**   
**1 cloves garlic**   
**1 cup basil**   
**1/2 tsp black pepper**   
**4 oz grated parmigiano**   
**2 tbl lemon juice**   
**1/4 tsp salt**   


2. Process until smooth. 
3. Cook **2 cup penne pasta** 
4. While the pasta cooks saute **1/4 cup minced shallot** . 
5. Add the pesto to the pan with the shallots until warmed through.
