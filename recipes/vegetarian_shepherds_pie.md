# Vegetarian Shepherd's Pie 

### Ingredients: 
* 2 lbs russet
* 3 tbl butter
* 1/4 cup sour cream
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 3/4 cup cheddar
* 1 cup onion
* 1 cup carrots
* 1/2 cup celery
* 8 oz mushrooms
* 3 cloves garlic
* 1/2 cup red wine
* 1 tsp red wine vinegar
* 3 tbl tomato paste
* 2 cup vegetable broth
* 15 oz canalini beans

### Directions: 

#### Mashed Potato Topping {
* Time this to boil while you make the filling so the potatoes stay warm. 
1. Place **2 lbs russet , peeled and chopped** in a large pot and add enough water to cover. Bring to a low boil and cook until soft. Drain and set aside. 
2. Place the warm potatoes in a large bowl. Add **3 tbl butter** , **1/4 cup sour cream** and **1/2 tsp salt** . Mash until creamy. 
3. Fold in **3/4 cup shredded cheddar** . 

}

1. Dice **1 cup onion** , **1 cup carrots** , **1/2 cup celery** . Slice **8 oz mushrooms** . 
2. Saute veg until they begin soften 
3. Add **3 cloves garlic , minced** and cook until fragrant. 
4. Add **1/2 cup red wine** and **1 tsp red wine vinegar** . Stir and scrape up any fond. 
5. Cook until most of the liquid has evaporated. 
6. Add **3 tbl tomato paste** , **2 cup vegetable broth** , **15 oz canalini beans**
