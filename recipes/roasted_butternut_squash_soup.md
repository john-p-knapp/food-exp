# Roasted Butternut Squash Soup 

### Ingredients: 
* 1 butternut squash
* 1 red onion
* 2 carrot
* 3 bell peppers
* 1 head garlic
*  oil
* 1 tsp Black Pepper
* 1 tsp ground cumin
* 1 tsp paprika
* 1 tsp dried thyme
* 1 tsp dried rosemary
* 1 tsp chili flakes
* 1.5 cup vegetable broth
* 1 tsp ginger
* 7 oz coconut milk
*  cilantro
*  chili oil

### Directions: 
1. Preheat the oven to 400F. 
2. Peel and roughly chop **1 butternut squash** **1 red onion** , **2 carrot** . 
3. Stem and clean **3 bell peppers** . 
4. Top **1 head garlic** . 
5. Combine all vegetables on a sheet tray. Toss with **oil** **1 tsp Black Pepper** , **1 tsp ground cumin** , **1 tsp paprika** , **1 tsp dried thyme** , **1 tsp dried rosemary** , **1 tsp chili flakes** . 
6. Cover tightly with aluminum foil. 
7. Roast for 1.5 hours or until vegetables are golden and soft. 
8. Remove foil and roast for another few minutes to caramelize slightly. 
9. Remove garlic from the tray and tranfer the vegetables to a blender. Squeeze the garlic pulp into the blender. Add **1.5 cup vegetable broth** , **1 tsp peeled ginger** . Blend until smooth. 
10. Tranfer soup to a pot, add **7 oz coconut milk** and heat through. 
11. Garnish with **cilantro** and **chili oil** if desired.
