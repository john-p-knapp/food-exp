# Buttermilk White Beans With Eggs & Greens 

* https://food52.com/recipes/82876-white-beans-with-buttermilk-recipe
* Serves 4. Active about 1 hour 20 minutes, plus chilling the beans at least 30 minutes (ideally overnight). 
### Ingredients: 
*  olive oil
* 5 sprig thyme
* 1 yellow onion
* 5 cloves garlic
* 1/2 lbs white beans
* 5 cloves garlic
* 2 tsp salt
* 2 cup buttermilk
*  hearty greens
* 1/2 cup white wine
* 1/4 tsp chile flakes
* 4 eggs
*  pepper

### Directions: 
1. In a medium to large saucepan heat **olive oil** over medium heat. Add **5 sprig thyme** and fry until fragrant, about 1 minute. 
2. Add **1 yellow onion , thinly sliced** and **5 cloves garlic , minced** ; reduce the heat to low and sweat until tender, 5 to 7 minutes. 
3. Add **1/2 lbs dried white beans** and enough water to cover by 2 to 3 inches. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer and cook until the beans are completely tender and creamy and the water is mostly absorbed, 45 to 75 minutes. 
4. Off the heat, stir into the beans combine:  
**5 cloves garlic , minced**   
**2 tsp kosher salt**   
**2 cup buttermilk**   

then cover and chill in the fridge at least 30 minutes, ideally overnight. 
5. Before serving, remove the thyme sprigs and gently warm the beans over low heat. Taste and adjust the seasoning. 
6. In a large frying pan heat olive oil over high heat until almost smoking. Add **hearty greens** cut into ribbons and a big pinch of salt. Saute until bright in color and starting to soften. 
7. Add **1/2 cup white wine** and **1/4 tsp chile flakes** (optional) and let the wine evaporate by half. 
8. Reduce the heat to medium and make four divots in the greens. Drizzle in more olive oil and crack **4 large eggs** one into each divot. Season with salt and **pepper** . Cover and steam the eggs 5 to 6 minutes, checking toward the end so they do not overcook. 
9. Dish the beans into bowls, nestle a scoop of greens and an egg on top, drizzle with more olive oil, and serve on its own or with a thick slice of toast.
