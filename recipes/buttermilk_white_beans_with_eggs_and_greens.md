# Buttermilk Beans, Greens, and Eggs 

### Ingredients: 
* 1 yellow onion
* 5 cloves garlic
*  olive oil
* 5 sprig thyme
* 1/2 lbs dried white beans
* 5 cloves garlic
* 2 tsp salt
* 2 cup buttermilk
* 2 head kale
*  olive oil
*  salt
* 1/2 cup white wine
* 1/4 tsp allepo
*  eggs

### Directions: 
1. Prep **1 yellow onion , thinly sliced** and **5 cloves garlic , minced** . In a medium to large saucepan heat **olive oil** over medium heat. Add **5 sprig thyme** and fry until fragrant, about 1 minute. 
2. Add onion and garlic; reduce the heat to low and sweat until tender. 
3. Add **1/2 lbs dried white beans** and enough water to cover by 2 inches. 
4. Simmer until the beans are completely tender and creamy and the water is mostly absorbed, 45 to 75 minutes. 
5. Off the heat, stir into the beans combine:  
**5 cloves garlic , minced**   
**2 tsp kosher salt**   
**2 cup buttermilk**   


6. Cover and chill in the fridge at least 30 minutes, ideally overnight. 
7. Before serving, remove the thyme sprigs and gently warm the beans over low heat. 
8. Prep **2 head kale** by washing and cutting into ribbons. 
9. Saute kale with **olive oil** and **salt** until bright in color and starting to soften. 
10. Add **1/2 cup white wine** and **1/4 tsp allepo** . Let the wine evaporate by roughly half. 
11. Fry **eggs** (or preparation of your choosing). 
12. Dish the beans into bowls, nestle a scoop of greens and an egg on top.
