# Creamy Avocado Soup 

### Ingredients: 
* 12 oz silken tofu
* 1 cup water
* 2 cup romaine
* 1 cup frozen green peas
* 3 avocado
* 3 tbl lime juice
* 3 tbl white wine vinegar
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp black pepper
* 1/4 cup tomato
* 1/4 cup yellow bell pepper
* 2 tbl scallions
*  salt
*  pepper
*  olive oil

### Directions: 
1. In blender combine:  
**12 oz silken tofu**   
**1 cup water**   
**2 cup romaine**   
**1 cup frozen green peas**   
**3 avocado**   
**3 tbl lime juice**   
**3 tbl white wine vinegar**   
**1 tsp salt**   
**1/2 tsp black pepper**   


2. Blend until smooth. 

#### Garnish {
3. Chop and combine:  
**1/4 cup tomato**   
**1/4 cup yellow bell pepper**   


4. Slice and add **2 tbl scallions** . 
5. Season with **salt** and **pepper** . 

}

1. Serve soup with **Garnish** and a drizzle of **olive oil** .
