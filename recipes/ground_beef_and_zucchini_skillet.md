# Ground Beef and Zucchini Skillet 

### Ingredients: 
* 1 sweet potato
* 1 red onion
* 1 zucchini
* 1 red pepper
* 4 cloves garlic
* 1 lbs ground beef
* 15 oz fire-roasted tomatoes
* 1 cup corn
* 15 oz black beans
* 3 tbl taco seasoning
* 1 cup monterey jack
*  cilantro
*  green onions

### Directions: 
1. Prepare: peal and 1/2 inch cube **1 sweet potato** . Dice **1 red onion** , **1 zucchini** , **1 red pepper** . Mince **4 cloves garlic** . 
2. In a large pan, saute sweet potato, stirring occasionally, for 4 to 5 minutes. 
3. Add remaining prepared veg and cook until the vegetables are slightly tender. 
4. Move the vegetables to one side of the skillet and place **1 lbs ground beef** on the other side. 
5. Cook the beef breaking it into small pieces as it cooks. 
6. Drain any excess grease once the beef is cooked through. 
7. Reduce the heat to medium-low 
8. Add **15 oz fire-roasted tomatoes** , **1 cup frozen corn** , **15 oz black beans , drained and rinsed** , and **3 tbl taco seasoning** . 
9. Sprinkle **1 cup shredded monterey jack** over the top, 
10. Place the lid on the skillet, remove from the heat, and let the cheese melt. 
11. Serve with **cilantro** and **green onions** .
