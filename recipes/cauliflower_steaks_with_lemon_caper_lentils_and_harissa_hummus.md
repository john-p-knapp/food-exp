# Cauliflower Steaks with Lemon-Caper Lentils and Harissa Hummus 

### Ingredients: 
* 1 cup black lentils
* 3 cup vegetable broth
* 1 tbl lemon juice
* 2 tbl capers
*  salt
*  pepper
* 1 cauliflower
* 2 tbl oil
*  salt
*  pepper
* 2 tbl harissa
* 2 tbl harissa
* 2 cloves garlic
* 2 tbl lemon juice
* 3 tbl oil
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/4 tsp pepper
*  harissa
* 1/4 cup mint
* 1/4 cup pine nuts
*  olive oil

### Directions: 

#### Lentils {
1. Rinse **1 cup black lentils** . 
2. In a medium saucepan, combine lentils and **3 cup vegetable broth** ; bring to a boil. 
3. Reduce heat and simmer, partially covered, until lentils are soft, 30–40 minutes. 
4. Stir in **1 tbl lemon juice** and **2 tbl capers** . 
5. Season to taste with **salt** and **pepper** . 

}


#### Cauliflower Steaks {
1. Preheat oven to 425 F. 
2. Clean **1 cauliflower** and cut into steaks. Place steaks and any stray florets on a rimmed baking sheet; Brush with **2 tbl oil** , and season with **salt** , and **pepper** . 
3. Bake for 15 minutes and then carefully flip the steaks; and bake for 10-15 minutes more. 
4. Brush with **2 tbl harissa** ; bake for 5 minutes. 

}


#### Harrisa Hummus {
1. In a food processor or blender combine:  
**2 tbl harissa**   
**2 cloves garlic**   
**2 tbl lemon juice**   
**3 tbl oil**   
**1/2 tsp salt**   
**1/4 tsp pepper**   

; blend until smooth. 

}

1. Divide harissa hummus evenly among each of 4 plates. Top with lentils and cauliflower. Brush with additional **harissa** over cauliflower, if desired. 
2. Garnish with **1/4 cup mint** , **1/4 cup pine nuts** , and an extra drizzle of **olive oil** .
