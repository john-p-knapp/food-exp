# Butternut Lentil Stew 

### Ingredients: 
* 1 yellow onion
* 1/2 butternut squash
* 2 tbl ginger
* 4 cloves garlic
* 3 tbl olive oil
* 2 tsp garam masala
* 1 tsp turmeric
* 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
* 3 cup water
*  salt
*  pepper
* 14 oz coconut milk
* 1 cup cooked lentils
* 3 cup kale
* 1/3 cup cilantro

### Directions: 
1. Prepare: dice **1 yellow onion** and **1/2 butternut squash** , grate **2 tbl ginger** , mince **4 cloves garlic** , 
2. In a dutch oven heat **3 tbl olive oil** saute onion until soft. 
3. Add ginger, garlic, squash. cook until fragrant, about 2 minutes. 
4. Stir in **2 tsp garam masala** , **1 tsp turmeric** and **1/2 tsp cayenne pepper** and bloom. 
5. Add **3 cup water** . Season with **salt** and **pepper** and bring to a simmer. 
6. Simmer until the butternut is tender. 
7. Stir in **14 oz coconut milk** , **1 cup cooked lentils** and **3 cup chopped kale** , and simmer until kale softens 5 
8. Remove from the heat and stir in **1/3 cup chopped cilantro** .
