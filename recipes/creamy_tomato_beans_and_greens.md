# Creamy Tomato Beans and Greens 

### Ingredients: 
* 1 onion
* 4 cloves garlic
*  salt
*  pepper
* 1/2 tsp allepo
* 1/3 cup tomato paste
* 28 oz cannellini beans
* 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes
* 1 cup water
* 1 cup heavy cream
* 1/3 cup Pecorino
* 4 cup baby arugula
* 2 tsp lemon zest
* 4 tsp lemon juice
* 1/3 cup Pecorino
* 2 tbl olive oil
*  sourdough

### Directions: 
1. Mince **1 onion** , **4 cloves garlic** 
2. Saute onion and garile; **salt** , **pepper** , and **1/2 tsp allepo** . Cook until softened. 
3. Add **1/3 cup tomato paste** and bloom. 
4. Add **28 oz cannellini beans , rinsed** , **1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes** and **1 cup water** , and simmer until beans are creamy and the mixture is moderately thick. 
5. Add **1 cup heavy cream** , **1/3 cup finely grated Pecorino** ; heat through; adjust seasoning. 
6. In a medium bowl, toss **4 cup baby arugula** , **2 tsp finely grated lemon zest** and **4 tsp lemon juice** , plus **1/3 cup finely grated Pecorino** and **2 tbl olive oil** ; season with salt and pepper. 
7. Pile the greens at the center of the beans. Serve with toasted **sourdough** .
