# Miso-Maple Chicken With Brussels Sprouts 

### Ingredients: 
* 1/3 cup white miso
* 1/3 cup maple syrup
* 1/3 cup olive oil
* 1 tsp ground cumin
* 12 oz brussels sprouts
* 2 lbs chicken breast

### Directions: 
1. Heat the oven to 425 degrees on convection roast. 
2. In a large bowl combine:  
**1/3 cup white miso**   
**1/3 cup maple syrup**   
**1/3 cup olive oil**   
**1 tsp ground cumin**   

; emulsify. 
3. Trim and half **12 oz brussels sprouts** ; add to bowl. 
4. Cut **2 lbs chicken breast** into 1.5 inch pieces; add to bowl. 
5. Toss all ingredients together. 
6. Add everything to the baking sheet, arranging in a single layer. Scrape any marinade left in the bowl over the mixture on the baking sheet. 
7. Bake until chicken is cooked through sprouts are lightly charred.
