# Sheet-Pan Gnocchi With Sausages and Peppers 

### Ingredients: 
* 1 onion
* 3 bell pepper
* 2 cloves garlic
* 1 cup cherry tomatoes
* 1 tsp dried oregano
* 1/2 tsp red chile flakes
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/4 cup olive oil
* 1 lbs hot italian suasage
* 1/4 cup basil
* 1/4 cup parmesan

### Directions: 
1. Heat oven to 425 degrees; convection bake. 
2. Thinly slice **1 onion** , **3 bell pepper** , **2 cloves garlic** 
3. On a sheet tray add sliced veg and combine:  
**1 cup cherry tomatoes**   
**1 tsp dried oregano**   
**1/2 tsp red chile flakes**   
**1 tsp salt**   
**1/4 cup olive oil**   

; Toss everything to coat. Distribute evenly. 
4. Prick **1 lbs hot italian suasage** and nestle into the veg. 
5. Roast for 15 minutes. Mix and flip suasages. 
6. Roast for 20 minutes more and test for doneness. 
7. Top with **1/4 cup basil , chopped** , and **1/4 cup parmesan** .
